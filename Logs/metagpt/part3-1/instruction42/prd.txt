# Resume Writing Service PRD

## 1. Language & Project Info
- **Language:** English
- **Project Name:** resume_writing_service
- **Restate the original requirements:** Create a polished, ATS-friendly resume for a Network/RF Engineer, emphasizing achievements and skills in Python automation, with deliverables including Word & PDF versions of the resume and an optional LinkedIn summary.

## 2. Product Definition
### Product Goals:
1. Create a polished, ATS-friendly resume tailored for Network/RF Engineers.
2. Highlight achievements and skills in Python automation effectively.
3. Provide deliverables in both Word and PDF formats, along with an optional LinkedIn summary.

### User Stories:
1. As a Network/RF Engineer, I want a resume that showcases my skills in Python automation so that I can attract potential employers.
2. As a job seeker, I want my resume to be ATS-friendly to ensure it passes through automated screening systems.
3. As a professional, I want my resume to be available in both Word and PDF formats for easy submission.

### Competitive Analysis:

1. **TopResume**  
   - **Pros:** Offers free resume reviews, extensive resources for job seekers, and a user-friendly interface.  
   - **Cons:** Higher pricing compared to some competitors, limited customization options.

2. **ResumeGenius**  
   - **Pros:** Provides templates specifically designed for ATS compatibility, easy-to-use resume builder.  
   - **Cons:** Limited personal touch in writing, may not cater specifically to engineers.

3. **Zety**  
   - **Pros:** Offers a wide range of templates and customization options, includes cover letter builder.  
   - **Cons:** Subscription model can be costly, some users report issues with customer service.

4. **LiveCareer**  
   - **Pros:** Provides a comprehensive suite of job search tools, including resume and cover letter builders.  
   - **Cons:** Some templates may not be ATS-friendly, pricing can be confusing.

5. **Resumake**  
   - **Pros:** Free to use, simple and straightforward interface, no sign-up required.  
   - **Cons:** Limited features compared to paid services, fewer templates available.

### Requirements Analysis:

1. **ATS Compatibility:** The resume must be formatted to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) without issues.
2. **User-Friendly Interface:** The service should provide an intuitive interface for users to input their information easily.
3. **Multiple Formats:** The ability to generate resumes in both Word and PDF formats is essential.
4. **Customization Options:** Users should have the ability to customize their resumes to highlight specific skills and achievements.
5. **LinkedIn Summary:** An optional feature to create a LinkedIn summary based on the resume content.
