## Implementation approach

We will create a command-line toolkit in Python that allows users to convert and manipulate CSV files. The toolkit will utilize the built-in `csv` module for reading and writing CSV files, and `argparse` for handling command-line arguments. We will also implement error handling to manage invalid inputs and provide a user-friendly interface.
